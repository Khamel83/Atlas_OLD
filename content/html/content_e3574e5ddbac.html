About the Patient-Led Research Collaborative

Join the PLRC Registry

The PLRC Registry is an user-friendly registry platform, designed by patients for patients, to connect people with clinical trials and research studies in Long COVID and associated conditions.

Join the PLRC Registry Today

Vision

We envision a world in which all people with Long COVID and associated conditions receive high quality care that improves their quality of life, and where research into Long COVID and associated conditions is equitable, patient-centered, efficient, and transformative.

Mission

Our mission is to improve the breadth, depth, and speed of global research into Long COVID and associated conditions, and to advocate for policies that improve the quality of life for Long COVID patients worldwide.

Who We Are

We are an international group of people with Long COVID and infection-associated chronic conditions who come from a diverse range of professional backgrounds. PLRC aims to represent people worldwide with Long COVID.

Our Impact

PLRC has influence beyond the community it represents. Our work impacts patients and the general public; health care professionals; researchers in academia, government and industry; policy makers in several governmental sectors, including public health agencies and drug regulation; and pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies.
