The film is losing 542 theaters this weekend, a 27% drop for a still-impressive 1,443-auditorium count in its 12th weekend. It should earn around $1.9 million this weekend in North America for a new $851.4 million domestic cume. The odds are still against it passing Avatar &#x27;s $2.788 billion global cume, but it certainly got within spitting distance, with a likely global cume as of Sunday of around $2.776 billion. It earned $3.1 million in North America last weekend but &quot;just&quot; $1.3 million overseas. The reissue may have goosed it a little bit over here, but the film is essentially done outside of North America. It&#x27;s been done for awhile in China, where it earned a ridiculous $614.3 million. To the extent that Avengers: Endgame got anywhere near Avatar , it&#x27;s mostly about China. Avengers: Infinity War earned $678.8 million in North America, $359.5 million in China and $1.01 billion over the rest of the world. That Infinity War earned so much WITHOUT North America or China is among its most impressive stats. Star Wars: The Force Awakens &#x27; &quot;no China/no America&quot; total was &quot;only&quot; $1.007 billion overseas. Comparatively, Avengers: Endgame has $850 million domestic, $614 million in China and $1.31 billion everywhere else. That&#x27;s still an insane overseas cume sans China.
