He was 86. The director-producer died Saturday, according to his son, actor Morten Holst. The cause of death was not immediately available. As a producer, Holst&#8217;s credits include films by von Trier ( The Element of Crime ), Nils Malmros ( Tree of Knowledge, Beauty and the Beast, Arhus by Night, Barbara and Paion of Love ) and Bille August ( Zappa , Twist &amp; Shout and Pelle the Conqueror, which won the 1989 Oscar for best foreign-language film). He also directed the 1985 film Walter and Carlo &mdash; Up on Daddy&#8217;s Hat, which is one of the highest-grossing Danish movies of all time. Holst also served as chairman of the board of the European Film College from 2000-05 and served on the Danish Film Institute&rsquo;s Feature Film Committee for several years. Throughout his career, he received several awards, including the Honorary Bodil in 1985, the Honorary Robert in 1995, LO&rsquo;s Culture Prize in 1998 and the Ib Prize in 2019. Holst is survived by his wife and four sons.
