The video features an audio recording of a long string of dots and dashes &mdash; which happens to be the script of the eighth Mission: Impossible movie played in Morse code. &#8220;A secure livestream of Mission: Impossible &mdash; The Final Reckoning full movie is running now,&#8221; reads the cheeky description of the video. It goes on to reference the rogue AI that IMF aims to destroy in the movie series: &#8220;But the Entity has infiltrated every major streaming platform, so this livestream was compromised. The full script had to be transmitted in Morse Code to avoid detection. We need your help decoding it. Your mission begins now.&#8221; Comments for the YouTube video have been turned off, but responses to the X post suggest that users got a kick out of the stunt that was timed to National Radio Day. &#8220;Peak trolling,&#8221; wrote one user who added a crying emoji. Love it lol.&#8221; Director Christopher McQuarrie &#8216;s movie hit theaters in May and was billed as Cruise&#8217;s farewell to character Ethan Hunt.
