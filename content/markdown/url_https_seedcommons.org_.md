Our investments are grounded in principles that set us apart from the traditional financial system. Our lending is designed, from the ground up, to be non-extractive . We deploy financial resources that advance workplace democracy and build community capacity . We leverage the deep connections and local expertise of a nationwide cooperative network . We center the workers and communities most excluded by the current system.
