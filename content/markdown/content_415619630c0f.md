This smart contract program runs on the Ethereum blockchain which means that as a user you will have an account that has a balance and you will be able to send transactions over the network, however, they are not controlled by the account holder, but instead are deployed to the network and run as programmed.</p><p>Now that you have a better idea of coding works, next we will look at how you can solidify yourself a spot amongst the stars.</p><h3>Blockchain Immortalization</h3><p>Most NFTs (Non-fungible tokens) are part of the Ethereum blockchain.
