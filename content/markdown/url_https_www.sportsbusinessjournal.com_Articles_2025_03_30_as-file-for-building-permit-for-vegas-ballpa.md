That application is “still being reviewed.” The A’s “must spend” the first $100M on the project before up to $380M in public funding from Nevada and Clark County is made available. A’s officials mentioned that they have “already spent north of” $50M on the ballpark. The work tied to the foundation and utility work will “push the amount spent on the project” by the A’s closer to the $100M mark. Plans call for that work to “kick off sometime between April and June” ( LAS VEGAS REVIEW-JOURNAL, 3/28 ).
