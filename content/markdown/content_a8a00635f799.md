					<p>I don&#8217;t know how long this will last but there are <strong>great deals on Etihad Airways flights</strong>.  You could fly from New York to Hong Kong for ~$373.  Or Chicago to Abu Dhabi for ~$211.</p>
<p>There are also great deals flying from the west coast.  And from Dallas to Mumbai.</p>

<p>I&#8217;ll explain how to book these fares!</p>
<p><span id="more-107312"></span></p>
<h3>Where You Can Fly &amp; How to Book</h3>
<p>Folks on <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/http://www.flyertalk.com/forum/mileage-run-deals/1640024-jfk-auh-187-rt-ai-7.html" target="_blank">Flyertalk</a> have reported success finding <strong>very low fares on Etihad.  </strong>One example was New York City (JFK) to Johannesburg (JNB) round-trip for ~$276 in March 2015.</p>
<figure id="attachment_107682" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_02.jpg"><img class="wp-image-107682 size-full" title="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" src="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_02.jpg" alt="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" width="600" height="431" srcset="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_02.jpg 600w, http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_02-300x215.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px"/></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Safari in South Africa This Winter for Under $300 Round-Trip on Etihad!</figcaption></figure>
<p>These deals from New York to Johannesburg are also available in other months.  You can use <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://www.google.com/flights/" target="_blank">Google Flights</a> to see a calendar with prices.</p>
<figure id="attachment_107683" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_03.jpg"><img class="wp-image-107683 size-full" title="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" src="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_03.jpg" alt="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" width="600" height="410" srcset="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_03.jpg 600w, http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_03-300x205.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px"/></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Google Flights Is Showing Cheap Airfare From Dallas to India in the Fall</figcaption></figure>
<p>Here&#8217;s my post for <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/2014/05/20/google-flights-search/" target="_blank">how to search airfare using Google Flights</a>.</p>
<p>Remember when you <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/Orbitz/" target="_blank">book on Orbitz</a> you have <strong>24 hours to cancel for free.  </strong>So you can find a flight you like, book it, then continue searching for a better flight.</p>
<figure id="attachment_107684" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_04.jpg"><img class="wp-image-107684 size-full" title="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" src="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_04.jpg" alt="Book NOW Incredibly Low Airfare To Hong Kong Abu Dhabi More" width="600" height="261" srcset="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_04.jpg 600w, http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344im_/https://millionmilesecrets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Book_NOW_Incredibly_Low_Airfare_To_Hong_Kong_Abu_Dhabi_More_04-300x130.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px"/></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Fly From Chicago to Abu Dhabi for ~$211! You Have 24 Hours to Cancel Your Flight When You Book on Orbitz</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Note:</strong>  Do NOT expect to earn miles in US-based programs like American Airlines.</p>
<h3 style="text-align: left;">Book Quickly, but&#8230;</h3>
<p style="text-align: left;">You should book this quickly.  <span style="color: #000000;">Low</span> fares don&#8217;t last long.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">But, do <strong>NOT</strong> book non-refundable hotel or other travel plans until you receive a confirmation <strong>WITH</strong> an <strong>e-ticket number. </strong> And remember:  <strong>DO</strong> <strong>NOT</strong> call the airline<span style="color: #000000;">!</span></p>
<h3>Which Card to Use?</h3>
<p>Don&#8217;t forget to use your <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/SapphirePreferredVisa/" target="_blank">Chase Sapphire Preferred</a> (which earns 2X points on travel) or <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/Arrival/" target="_blank">Barclaycard Arrival Plus</a> (which earns 2.1% back on travel).</p>
<p>There&#8217;s nothing better than finding a low fare <strong>and</strong> getting bonus points for the ticket!</p>
<h3>Bottom Line</h3>
<p>There are great deals on <strong>Etihad</strong> flights right now.  I don&#8217;t how long this will last!</p>
<p>Use <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://www.google.com/flights/" target="_blank">Google Flights</a> to search for trips.  Then book using <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20170724034344/https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/Orbitz/" target="_blank">Orbitz</a> to take advantage of their 24 hour cancelation policy.</p>
<p>Let me know if you book a flight!</p>
