It is time to recognize this as a chronic condition rather than a temporary event. Current strategies have not placed transit on a financially sustainable path. From the mid-19th century through the mid-20th, transit was privately operated, usually running on public rights-of-way (which companies often were obligated to maintain), charging a government-regulated fare.
