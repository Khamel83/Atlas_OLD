Subscribe wherever you get your podcasts.All EpisodesDavid Brooks on Audacity, AI, and the American Psyche (Live at 92NY)David Brooks on Audacity, AI, and the American Psyche (Live at 92NY)Aug 20, 2025David Brooks returns to the show with a stark
diagnosis of American culture. Having evolved from a Democratic
socialist to a neoconservative to what he now calls "the rightward
edge of the leftward tendency," Brooks argues that America's core
problems aren't economic but sociological—rooted in the destruction
of our "secure base" of family, community, and moral order that
once gave people existential security.Tyler and David cover why young people are
simultaneously the most rejected and most productive generation,
smartphones and sex, the persuasiveness of AI vs novels, the loss
of audacity, what made William F. Buckley and Milton Friedman great
mentors, why academics should embrace the epistemology of the
interview, the evolving status of neoconservatism, what Trump gets
right, whether only war or mass movements can revive the American
psyche, what will end the fertility crisis, the subject of his
book, listener questions, and much more.Read
