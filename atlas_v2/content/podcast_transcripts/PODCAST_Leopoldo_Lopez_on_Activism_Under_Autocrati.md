Subscribe wherever you get your podcasts.All EpisodesLeopoldo López on Activism Under Autocratic RegimesLeopoldo López on Activism Under Autocratic RegimesJul 27, 2022As an inquisitive reader, books were a cherished commodity for
Leopoldo López when he was a political prisoner in his home country
of Venezuela. His prison guards eventually observed the strength
and focus López gained from reading. In an attempt to stifle his
spirit, the guards confiscated his books and locked them in a
neighboring cell where he could see but not access them. But López
didn’t let this stop him from writing or discourage his resolve to
fight for freedom. A Venezuelan opposition leader and freedom
activist, today López works to research and resist oppressive
autocratic regimes globally.López joined Tyler to discuss Venezuela’s recent political and
economic history, the effectiveness of sanctions, his experiences
in politics and activism, how happiness is about finding purpose,
how he organized a protest from prison, the ideal daily routine of
a political prisoner, how extreme sports prepared him for prison,
his work to improve the lives of the Venezuelan people, and
more.Read afull
