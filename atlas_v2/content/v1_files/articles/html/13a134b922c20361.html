Get Another Year of Las Vegas VIP Line Night Club Access, Buffet Line Passes & More

If you have Hyatt elite status, you may have been enjoying the perks of elite status at MGM hotels in Las Vegas.  But the year’s almost up and it’s time to renew your M life (MGM Resorts’ loyalty program) elite status.

Don’t Let Your VIP Benefits Expire

And if you don’t have elite status, I’ll tell you how to get it and how to match your status to M life.

Hyatt and M Life Partnership

Link:   Hyatt Gold Passport program

Link:   M life program

You get the benefits of both memberships when you at stay at M life hotels in Las Vegas, if you’re a Hyatt Gold Passport member AND a M life member.

M life hotels in Las Vegas include:

  • Aria
  • Bellagio
  • Delano
  • Excalibur
  • Luxor
  • Mandalay Bay
  • MGM Grand
  • Mirage
  • Monte Carlo
  • New York, New York
  • The Signature at MGM Grand
  • Vdara

You Get VIP Benefits at These 12 M Life Resorts

VIP Benefits for Hyatt Elite Status Members

If you have Hyatt Platinum elite status, you can match your elite status to M life Gold status.  Hyatt Diamond members can match to M life Platinum status.

Your Hyatt Status Gets You Matching M life Status

M life Gold status gets you:

  • Room upgrades
  • Priority check-in
  • Priority Fine dining reservations
  • VIP Line Night Club access
  • 10% shopping discount

M life Platinum status gets you:

  • Room upgrades
  • Priority check-in
  • Turndown service
  • Instant access to pool cabanas and bungalows
  • Concierge phone line
  • First access to spa and salons reservations
  • Priority Fine dining reservations
  • VIP Line Night Club access
  • Priority valet and taxi service
  • 15% shopping discount

Match or Renew Your Hyatt Elite Status to M life

To match or keep your M life Gold or Platinum status (if you matched last year), you have to opt-in again.  But it’s easy to match your Hyatt elite status to M life.

Here’s my step-by-step guide to matching your Hyatt elite status to M life.

My Hyatt Diamond Elite Status Was Matched to M life Platinum Elite Status

How to Get Hyatt Elite Status

If you don’t have Hyatt elite status, here’s how you can get it.

Hyatt Card

Link:   Hyatt card

Link:   My review of the Hyatt card

Link:   Hyatt Platinum benefits

You get Hyatt Platinum elite status for as long as you have the Hyatt card.

Hyatt Platinum elite status gets you:

  • Free internet
  • Room upgrades (when available)
  • 20% off the daily rate at My Elite Rate (when available)
  • Priority check-in
  • Late check-out

With the Hyatt card you also get:

  • 2 free nights at any Hyatt worldwide after spending $1,000 in the 1st 3 months
  • 1 free night in Category 1 to 4 Hyatt every card anniversary
  • NO foreign transaction fees

The $75 annual fee is waived the first year.

You Can Use Your 2 Free Nights From the Hyatt Card at the Park Hyatt Milan

Buying a Ticket to Frequent Traveler University

Link:   Frequent Traveler University, Washington DC

Link:   FAQs for Frequent Traveler University Hyatt Platinum Status 

You can get Hyatt Platinum elite status when you buy a ticket to Frequent Traveler University, Washington DC.

Frequent Traveler University is a 1 of the best places to learn about miles and points and meet other like-minded folks.

Frequent Traveler University, Washington DC will be held December 5, 2014, to December 7, 2014 at the Hyatt Regency Crystal City.

You can spend the weekend learning about miles and points.  Here are all the details of the largest Frequent Traveler University ever!

Get Hyatt Platinum Elite Status When You Buy Your Ticket to Frequent Traveler University Washington, DC – December 5, 2014 to December 7, 2014

Hyatt Diamond Challenge

Link:   Hyatt Diamond benefits

Link:   Hyatt Diamond challenge

You get Hyatt Diamond elite status after 25 stays or 50 nights in a calendar year.

As a Hyatt Diamond, you get:

  • Free continental breakfast (full breakfast in hotels without a club lounge)
  • Free internet
  • 4 suite upgrades a year
  • Free upgrades
  • Late check-out (4:00 pm)

You Get Free Breakfast (Some Hotels Include Room Service Breakfast) as a Hyatt Diamond

But there’s a shortcut to Hyatt Diamond status, the Hyatt Diamond challenge!

If you have elite status with another hotel and stayed at least 1 time with that hotel, you can request a Hyatt Diamond challenge by emailing Hyatt Gold Passport at gold passport@hyatt.com or calling Hyatt at 800-304-9288.

Bottom Line

You have to opt-in again to match your Hyatt elite status with M life elite status.

Hyatt Platinum elite status gets you M life Gold status.  And Hyatt Diamond members match to M life Platinum status.

M life Gold and M life Platinum status gets you perks like room upgrades, priority check-in, and VIP Line Night Club access.

Here’s my step-by-step guide to matching your Hyatt elite status to M life.

You can get Hyatt Platinum status from the Hyatt card or buying a ticket to Frequent Traveler University, Washington, DC.

You can get Hyatt Diamond status quickly with the Hyatt Diamond challenge.

Don’t forget to renew your M life status.
