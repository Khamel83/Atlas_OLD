#  Automated Tinder and the Eigenface

Because using Tinder takes up oh so much time swiping, swiping, and swiping,
[Justin Long made a bot that swipes and starts conversations for
him](http://crockpotveggies.com/2015/02/09/automating-tinder-with-
eigenfaces.html). Step 1: Use his existing preferences to create two
Eigenfaces, commonly used in face recognition, that represent a yes and a no.
Step 2: Automate everything else with the Tinder API.

> What are the results so far? The bot is amazingly effective. I would
> estimate an accuracy of up to 70% in its selections – though there may be a
> hindsight bias. Using a brand new account, I did a quick test to see how
> quickly the bot could get results. In 48 hours, the bot registered 21
> matches (starting all of those conversations), made 4 extended
> conversations, and the bot itself made over 300 moves. A “move” is any step
> the bot makes in either sending a message or making a swipe. And in that
> time I barely needed to touch the app. I also created a dashboard to give me
> an overview of my metrics.

Kids these days.
