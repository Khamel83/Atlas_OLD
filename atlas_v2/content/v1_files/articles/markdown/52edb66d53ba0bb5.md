island in the Russian arctic, but still "valid" in the sense that north-at-
the-top is a convention, not a scientific principle.

This map was created by Stuart MacArthur of Melbourne, Australia, and is

[commercially
available](http://web.archive.org/web/20231121234141/http://www.odt.org/NewMaps.htm#Mcarthur)
