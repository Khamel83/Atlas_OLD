Here's a deal we don't see every day. Several carriers are offering great
fares on flights to Cairo, Egypt (CAI). Fly from either New York (JFK) or
Chicago (ORD) to the Egyptian capital from just $550 round-trip on Air France.
New York-based flyers have a choice between Egyptair's nonstop flight from JFK
to CAI, or can fly via Paris (CDG) on Air France for a few dollars less. If
you're based in Chicago (ORD), you'll find the low fares on Etihad, so you'll
have to connect in Abu Dhabi (AUH). These prices are generally available from
November 2017 all the way through May of 2018, but exact availability depends
on where you're originating from. Use [Google
Flights](http://web.archive.org/web/20230203205222/https://go.skimresources.com/?id=108360X1565867&sref=https%3A%2F%2Fthepointsguy.com%2Fnews%2Fdeal-
egypt-from-550&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.google.com%2Fflights%2F) to find dates
that work for you, and then either book through the airlines directly or
through an OTA like Expedia or Orbitz.

**Airlines:** Air France, Egyptair, Etihad
**Routes:** JFK/ORD to CAI
**Cost:** $550+ round-trip in economy
**Dates:** November 2017 - May 2018
**Booking Link:** Orbitz or**** Expedia
**Pay With:** The Platinum Card from American Express (5x on airfare), Chase
Sapphire Reserve, Premier Rewards Gold Card from American Express, Citi
Prestige (3x on airfare) or Chase Sapphire Preferred (2x on travel)
