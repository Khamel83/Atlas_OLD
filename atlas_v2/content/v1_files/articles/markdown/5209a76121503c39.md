The winners of the 2017 Epson International Pano Awards [have been
announced](http://web.archive.org/web/20241111005816/https://thepanoawards.com/).
In Focus has [a round-up of some of the best
ones](http://web.archive.org/web/20241111005816/https://www.theatlantic.com/photo/2017/11/winners-
of-the-2017-epson-international-pano-awards/546383/). It was tough to choose
just three to feature here, so make sure and check out all the winners. Photos
by Francisco Negroni, Paolo Lazzarotti, and Ray Jennings.
