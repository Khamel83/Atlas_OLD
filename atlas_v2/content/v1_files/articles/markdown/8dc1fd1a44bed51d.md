## 17 equations that changed the world

![17 Equations](/web/20250614112222im_/https://kottke.org/cdn-
cgi/image/format=auto,fit=scale-
down,width=1200,metadata=none/plus/misc/images/17-equations.jpg)

In the book [In Pursuit of the
Unknown](http://web.archive.org/web/20250614112222/http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B007P05A1W/ref=nosim/0sil8),
Ian Stewart discusses how equations from the likes of Pythagoras, Euler,
Newton, Fourier, Maxwell, and Einstein have been used to build the modern
world.

I love how as time progresses, the equations get more complicated and
difficult for the layperson to read (much less understand) and then Boltzmann
and Einstein are like, boom!, entropy is increasing and energy is proportional
to mass, suckas!
