For his recently released book [Wild
Life](http://web.archive.org/web/20240806021533/http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/3791348922/ref=nosim/0sil8),
Brad Wilson shot photos of all kinds of animals on a black background,
resulting in unusually expressive portraits.
