## Steven Soderbergh’s 2014 media diet

Once again, Steven Soderbergh kept track of every book, TV show, movie, play,
and short story [he read or watched in
2014](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/22-seen-
read-2014). A sampling: Girls, True Detective, Gone Girl, 2001 (3 times), Dr.
Strangelove, Olive Kitteridge, My Struggle: Book One, Boardwalk Empire, and
[his black & white version of Raiders of the Lost
Ark](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/18-raiders)
(twice).

Here are his lists for
[2013](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/12-seen-
read-2013) (House of Cards, Koyaanisqatsi),
[2012](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/9-seen-
read-2012) (This is Spinal Tap, The Lady in the Lake),
[2011](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/7-seen-
read-2011) (Cave of Forgotten Dreams, Senna),
[2010](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/5-seen-
read-2010) (Mad Men, Where Good Ideas Come From), and
[2009](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/http://extension765.com/sdr/3-seen-
read-2009) (Breaking Bad, Slap Shot). (via
[@khoi](http://web.archive.org/web/20250711190158/https://twitter.com/khoi))
