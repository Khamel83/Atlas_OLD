## Desire map of the 50 states

![What States Want](/web/20250522071122im_/https://kottke.org/cdn-
cgi/image/format=auto,fit=scale-
down,width=1200,metadata=none/plus/misc/images/what-states-want.jpg)

In Alaska, people search for the cost of a gallon of milk. In Alabama and
Florida, people search for the cost of abortions. In other states,
vasectomies, facelifts, and taxis are popular searches. [The
map](http://web.archive.org/web/20250522071122/http://www.fixr.com/blog/2015/02/27/cost-
obsessions-us-map/) was compiled using the autocomplete results for “how much
does a * cost”… for each of the 50 states. (via
[mr](http://web.archive.org/web/20250522071122/http://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2015/03/what-
cost-is-each-state-obsessed-with.html))
