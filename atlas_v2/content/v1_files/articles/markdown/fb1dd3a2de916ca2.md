## Mapper’s Delight

A project called [Maximum Distance. Minimum
Displacement.](http://web.archive.org/web/20241203115025/http://www.mappersdelight.net/)
analyzed the lyrics of several popular rappers for geographical mentions and
had an industrial robot draw each rapper’s lyrical journey through the world.
At a glance, you can see how worldly (N***as in Paris) or locally oriented
(Straight Outta Compton) each rapper is. Compare world-traveller [Jay
Z](http://web.archive.org/web/20241203115025/http://www.mappersdelight.net/?p=17):

> Kendrick Lamar’s analysis is culled from the lyrics of his underground &
> independent albums and is heavy with Compton references. Over the next few
> years it will be interesting to see how mainstream successes and personal
> experience change the travel of his lyrics.
