**We’ve updated our Snake Oil Supplements? interactive with the latest
scientific evidence for the health benefits of the 180 most popular
nutritional supps.**

The data has been revised top to bottom and draws upon many new
[Cochrane](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://cochranelibrary.com/)
metastudies of the area.

(Cochrane are the gold-standard of medical meta-studies – studies of studies.
They minimise bias by subjecting all existing studies to very rigorous
methodological and statistical analysis).

**»[See the
interactive](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/https://informationisbeautiful.net/visualizations/snake-
oil-supplements/)**

# our analysis

* * *

**NEW ENTRIES**

» **Melatonin for insomnia** – what travellers already knew is now proven
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/14651858.CD009776.pub2/abstract))
» **Dark chocolate for blood pressure** – small blood pressure drop
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/14651858.CD008893.pub2/abstract))
» **Olive Leaf extract for blood pressure** – evidence it’s good for heart
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://journals.cambridge.org/action/displayAbstract?fromPage=online&aid=9860730&fileId=S0007114515001269))
» **Rhodiola rosea L for fatigue** – may alleviate tiredness, more research
required
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.biomedcentral.com/1472-6882/12/70))
» **Vitamin K2 for heart disease** – may prevent heart calcification
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://ndt.oxfordjournals.org/content/early/2013/11/26/ndt.gft464.short))
» **Eggshell membrane for arthritis pain** – works fast at 500mg per day
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/o/cochrane/clcentral/articles/993/CN-00718993/frame.html))
» **Garlic for high cholesterol** – 600mg a day brings it down
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.cochrane.org/CD007653/HTN_garlic-
for-hypertension))
» **Vitamin D for flu, bones, and long life** – good evidence for this all-
rounder
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20219962))

* * *

**PROMOTIONS**

» **Devil’s Claw for lower back pain** – low evidence that it reduces pain
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/14651858.CD004504.pub4/abstract))
»**Zinc for colds** – reduces duration of cold at dose of ≥ 75 mg/day
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23775705))

* * *

**DEMOTIONS**

»**Probiotics for IBS** – evidence drops from strong to conflicting
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19277023))
» **Valerian for anxiety** – massive drop to only slight evidence
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.cochrane.org/CD004515/DEPRESSN_valerian-
for-anxiety-disorders))
» **Cranberry juice for urinary infections** – zero evidence for cystitis
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.cochrane.org/CD001321/RENAL_cranberries-
for-preventing-urinary-tract-infections))
» **Green tea for lowering cholesterol** – evidence was strong, now slim
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.cochrane.org/CD009934/VASC_green-
and-black-tea-to-prevent-cardiovascular-disease))
» **Fish Oil for heart disease** – the “miracle oil” continues its decline
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/14651858.CD003177.pub2/abstract))

* * *

**HARMFUL**

Thanks to visitor suggestions we’ve added a new category for supplements that
may be harmful.

» **Antioxidants linked to higher mortality** – with no evidence of health
benefits
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15153272))
» **Vitamin A linked to birth defects** – safe as a skin cream, though
([study](http://web.archive.org/web/20250529134801/http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9431575))
