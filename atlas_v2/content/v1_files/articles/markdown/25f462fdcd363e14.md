Christopher Nolan’s _Interstellar_ is absolutely gorgeous. _Interstellar_ has
strong performances and real tearjerking moments. Interstellar also has some
pretty large issues and (potential) plot holes that unravel over the course of
its nearly-three-hour runtime.

Senior Editor Ross Miller, Reports Editor Josh Dzieza, and Science Reporter
Arielle Duhaime-Ross sat down for a very special one-off audio podcast to
discuss their thoughts on _Interstellar_ and try to hash out some of the
bigger issues.
