**UPDATE:** As several readers pointed out, the original post was incorrect.
Originally I wrote that you could get Hertz President’s Circle status using
the methods described below. In reality, you will get Hertz Five Star status
which is a level below President’s Circle…

First United partnered up with Hertz, and [now Delta has done the
same](http://loyaltylobby.com/2014/12/15/hertz-gold-plus-rewards-five-star-or-
presidents-circle-status-for-delta-medallion-members/). Both programs offer a
way for elites to get elite status (Five Star or President’s Circle) with
Hertz, for free.

To qualify for [President’s Circle with United
status](http://ir.unitedcontinentalholdings.com/phoenix.zhtml?c=83680&p=irol-
newsArticle&ID=1979877) (starting Jan 1), you must have United Platinum status
(or higher), or be a primary cardholder of either: MileagePlus Club or
Presidential Plus Card. To qualify for **Hertz** **Five Star status** with,
you must have United Silver or Gold status.

To qualify for [President’s Circle with Delta
status](https://www.delta.com/mypromos/acelanding.action?offer=hertz_status_benefit)
(starting Jan 1), you must have Delta Platinum or Diamond status. To qualify
for **Hertz Five Star status** you must have Delta Gold status.

## An easy path for everyone else

If you don’t have high enough status with United or Delta to qualify for Hertz
~~President’s Circle~~ Five Star status, here’s a simple alternative:

## 1\. Sign up for free elite status with Avis and/or National

All you need is a World Elite MasterCard (like the BarclayCard Arrival Plus).
Then, click the following links and sign up (hat tip: [View from the
Wing](http://viewfromthewing.boardingarea.com/2014/08/10/get-instant-free-
status-avis-national-car-rental/)):

By signing up through the Avis link you will get a coupon for a free two day
rental!

## 2\. Status match to Hertz ~~President’s Circle~~ Five Star

Send an email requesting a status match to
[hertzstatusmatch@hertz.com](mailto:hertzstatusmatch@hertz.com). Include a
scanned picture of your elite card with Avis or National, or simply take a
screenshot of your Avis or National account. There are many recent examples on
[StatusMatcher.com](http://www.statusmatcher.com/company/21347) showing that
this works.

## Five Star Benefits

Personally, I’m a big fan of National Executive status ([see why,
here](https://frequentmiler.com/2014/08/18/discover-rental-car-discounts-and-
upgrades/)), but if you’re going to rent from Hertz, you might as well have
status. [Here’s what Hertz says about Five Star
status](https://www.hertz.com/rentacar/emember/join/overview.do?learnMore=fiveStar):

    * One-car-class upgrades (subject to availability)
    * 10% bonus on Hertz Gold Plus Rewards® Points
    * 675 Rewards Points after every 15 qualifying rentals
    * Plus, all Gold member benefits

And a comparison of the different levels of status can be found
[here](https://www.hertz.com/rentacar/emember/hertz-awards-sumGoldbenefits).
