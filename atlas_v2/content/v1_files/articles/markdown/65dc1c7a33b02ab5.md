### ![14674865190_3185001dcb_o](https://grantland.com/wp-

**BS:** I’m against man-made memorabilia in all forms … unless it’s an

**RB:** Thought about sawing this in half, throwing $300 on the ground, and
running away with the top half.

## ![14859083814_d139319c5c_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14859083814_d139319c5c_o.jpg)

**BS:** In 2011, they were selling this in a “Super Sale” for $975 and I made
the joke, “I’ll be there for yoooooooou … (for only twelve hundred bucks) …
I’ll be there for yoooooooooou … (I’ll lower it another two) … I’ll be there
for yooooooooou … (I’ll take seven but no lower) … ” In 2014? The exact same
item is going for under $900 and ‘priced at our lost.'” Tough times for the
man-made _Friends_ memorabilia market. Regardless, I’m taking this baby at the
2035 convention for $80. Mark it down.

**RB:** Advice to the human still trying to sell the _Friends_ collage: Trash
these six photos and come back next year with Janice, Gunther, Marcel, Ugly
Naked Guy, and Ursula.

## ![14881434273_1f327ae699_o](https://grantland.com/wp-

then you can trade Kennedys for Kennedys. As in, it’s public knowledge that
two sheets of RFK equal one mint-condition Eunice. That’s a world I want to
live in.

**BS:** We already get to live in a world with a Klosterman card. You want to
push your luck?

## ITEMS FROM THREE YEARS AGO THAT SADLY WEREN’T IN CLEVELAND

##
![](https://a.espncdn.com/photo/2011/0817/grantland_bs_collectibles_022.jpg)

**BS:** My favorite item from every collectors convention … and it’s gone!
MIA! Disappeared! What happened? Where is it? Why didn’t I buy it when I had
the chance? Did Mickey Mantle make any other ads in which he inadvertently

The Dip Receptacle of Champions.”

## MOST INTERESTING CONVENTION TRENDS

![14861219762_012ef661ea_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14861219762_012ef661ea_o1.jpg)

**RB:** What?

**BS:** The hockey collector selling these Gretzky rookies told me that
someone showed up and cleaned out “half the case,” then explained, “I dunno,
Gretzky’s hot right now.” Because that totally makes sense.

## ![14674873330_808717ed20_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674873330_808717ed20_o.jpg)

**BS:** Now this exploding market makes more sense …

![14861216622_6e149d5f31_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14861216622_6e149d5f31_o.jpg)

**BS:** See, unopened Topps boxes from the 1950s, 1960s and 1970s are blowing
up. Especially in football and basketball. Our friend at Baseball Card
Exchange said he moved so many NBA boxes during the VIP auction that he
eventually bumped the price of a 1987 Fleer basketball box from $1,500 (2013)
to $2,900 (2014). Those old Topps boxes were my favorite things to collect —
they look cool and they’re an incredible ongoing test of willpower (especially
when it’s 3 a.m. and you’re drunk).

**RB:** What?

## ![14861198772_53bd4891a8_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14861198772_53bd4891a8_o.jpg)

**BS:** The complete-uniform market is taking off — an important development
for grown men with disposable income who don’t think it’s already strange
enough that they’re wearing someone else’s jersey. _You want strange? Watch
this … I’m putting on the pants too!_

**RB:** If you see anyone not named “Tommy Amaker” wearing this, Obama will
pardon you should you decide to embarrass them in front of their closest
friends and family.

## ![14674842640_2ff67fdf7a_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674842640_2ff67fdf7a_o.jpg)

**RB:** BANDWAGON BOOTH ALERT.

**BS:** We knew soccer couldn’t make it in America until it had its own Sports
Collectors Convention booth that everyone ignored. Baby steps.

## THE BELATEDLY RACIST AND/OR OFFENSIVE AWARDS

## ![14881456063_1d945e9bd6_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14881456063_1d945e9bd6_o.jpg)

**RB:** One of my goals in life is to buy all the racist items at flea
markets, just to get them off the streets. You should see the amount of hate
in my storage unit.

**BS:** At the convention, you mastered the art of “being horrified by
something racist while simultaneously being delighted that the same item was
so absurdly over-the-top racist.”

**RB:** Still unclear why the artist colored O.J. in with charcoal. He looks
like a dusty The Thing.

## ![14706631437_00c6d94420_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14706631437_00c6d94420_o.jpg)

**BS:** “Merry Christmas — here’s the framed Hitler movie poster that I knew
you wanted.”

**RB:** The terrible thing is, at one point, I’m sure this was a gift.

## [![14858497571_f1f608687f_o](https://grantland.com/wp-

And by “once-racist,” I mean “right now.”

**RB:** If Dan Snyder sees this, Joe House might be the lucky recipient of a
big-time LinkedIn request (fingers crossed).

## ![14674902069_c1bb8e1fbc_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674902069_c1bb8e1fbc_o.jpg)

**BS:** This wasn’t offensive, just felt offensive. Please, don’t call him
Tarzan.

**RB:** I imagine this book is just filled with scenarios that could occur
should he be called Tarzan. Terrifyingly violent, unmerciful scenarios.

## ![14706540749_120cec5cf7_o](https://grantland.com/wp-

**BS:** Now THIS was offensive. We’ve come a long way, America.

**RB:** MediaTakeOut has been around for a long, long time.

## ![14674916798_52d2dd360a_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674916798_52d2dd360a_o.jpg)

**BS:** “Excuse me — does ‘highest graded’ mean the card, or just Mussolini on
the scale of evil dictators in general?”

**RB:** While understandably disrespectful, “Mint Mussolini” would be an
incredible rap name. Also: Something Rick Ross will certainly refer to himself
as in the next six months.

## ![14859072654_bd0547a78b_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14859072654_bd0547a78b_o.jpg)

**BS:** A scrapbook of German newspapers from before, during and after the
1936 Olympics in Berlin. This was definitely a one-of-a-kind piece. Even if
there’s no good way to explain collecting stuff from the 1936 Olympics short
of saying, “I am a huge, huge, HUGE weirdo.”

**RB:** Yeah, “weirdo,” except rearrange the letters so it spells “fascist.”

![14858465221_9411db7410_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14858465221_9411db7410_o.jpg)

**BS:** We enjoyed this particular page because German shooting gold medalist
Cornelius van Oyen (bottom right) was dressed kinda like Joe House that day.
That’s right: the collectors convention — where you make jokes about your
friends dressing like Nazis without anyone’s feelings being hurt. Sign me up
for next year!
