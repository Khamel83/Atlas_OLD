## BEST POP-CULTURE ITEMS THAT WE NEVER CONSIDERED BUYING

## ![14674889630_ce2fc047e2_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674889630_ce2fc047e2_o.jpg)

**BS:** That’s a Prince concert program from the late ’80s. I don’t know if
the look on Rem’s face is shock, awe, respect, horror, affection … I just
can’t tell.

**RB:** Fear. Jealousy. Friendship.

## ![14674954978_647e9af6de_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674954978_647e9af6de_o.jpg)

**RB:** Really wanted to buy this Jimi Hendrix cardboard coat hanger. The
greatest Christmas tree ornament I’ve ever seen.

**BS:** You could have talked me into the price being $3, $30, $300 or $3,000
and I would have believed any of those numbers. (It was $300.)

## ![14861586755_e2771d862e_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14861586755_e2771d862e_o.jpg)

**BS:** That’s an item autographed by The Guy Who Played Darth Vader for a
scant $129. He didn’t even speak! He just walked around in the Vader outfit!
If we ever want to boost Grantland’s traffic in the cheapest way possible, I
vote for _Star Wars_ Week.

**RB:** I wonder — what was the price before George Lucas decided to do _Red
Tails_?

## ![14884500194_b0a8f7d5d1_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14884500194_b0a8f7d5d1_o.jpg)

**BS:** So … why are we including a David Boreanaz photo in this photo essay,
Rem?

**RB:** [CLICK HERE](http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2009/12/04/tiger-woods-
dream-derek-j_n_380697.html) RIGHT NOW!

## ![14674878999_37e6716a51_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674878999_37e6716a51_o.jpg)

**BS:** I come to the convention to find one-of-a-kind pieces, pick them up,
then stare at them for two to three minutes as the seller hovers excitedly …
before ultimately walking away because I can’t figure out where the piece
would go other than the back of my garage.

**RB:** Would have bought this if the font were Garamond. But it’s not. So I
didn’t.

## ![14861517015_188a588fca_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14861517015_188a588fca_o.jpg)

**RB:** Again, that signature ruins everything. Completely messing up Ringo’s
white cardigan.

**BS:** Has there ever been a photo with more star power? Ali with the
Beatles? Is this the greatest photo ever taken? (Hold this thought.)

## ![14858498931_e9c07232e2_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14858498931_e9c07232e2_o.jpg)

**BS:** If I liked the Allman Brothers 39 percent more, I would have snapped
up that blue cardboard promo buggy of an album that wasn’t even one of their
best albums. This was a great booth run by a music collector named [Will
Simmons](http://www.beckett.com/news/2013/07/at-the-national-a-musical-
reprieve-from-sports-collectibles/) who had loads of one-of-a-kind stuff. Who
knew there were other white people with the last name “Simmons”?

**RB:** It’s your family, that guy, and Richard Simmons. No others. Don’t even
bother looking. Can we do a Simmons power rankings? Unclear where you fall,
but Sue is still no. 1.

VIDEO

**BS:** I have Sue third. It’s Russell Simmons, Gene Simmons, Sue Simmons, Ted
Simmons, Al Simmons … and I think I’m holding off on Andrelton Simmons for the
no. 6 spot until he learns to hit.

## ![14674894199_9cd464aced_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674894199_9cd464aced_o.jpg)

**BS:** I’ll trade you four Efron rookies for one Klosterman rookie.

**RB:** No can do, but may I interest you in a Mint Mussolini?

## ITEMS THAT STARTED IMPROMPTU CONVERSATIONS

## ![14700277380_390ed40a1a_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14700277380_390ed40a1a_o.jpg)

**BS:** The big four — Mandela, Liz, Michael, Jacoby.

**RB:** I love David Jacoby with all my heart, but I had no idea he was even
in this picture. But I’ll take your word for it.

## ![14859085564_5cc7ce5221_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14859085564_5cc7ce5221_o.jpg)

**BS:** Varitek & A-Rod, Est. 2004.

**RB:** The only way this is worse is if it’s an oil painting.

**BS:** Let’s tackle two photos that elicited prolonged stares AND forlorn
reactions from us.

## ![14700421947_23b3c85e47_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14700421947_23b3c85e47_o.jpg)

**RB:** I wish there were a way to buy this with the Joe House reflection
included. Then, and only then, do you get my $1,695.

## ![14674831870_8f68974313_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674831870_8f68974313_o.jpg)

**BS:** From 1987 through 1996, I and many other Boston fans would have
considered donating a kidney to own this giant Clemens 20K Game photo. And
even if I’d consider buying it now only if you also gave me the option to
legally break it over his head, the photo still gives me little tingles. I
can’t lie.

## ![14838541516_27e81e488e_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14838541516_27e81e488e_o.jpg)

**BS:** The collectors convention … a place where you have conversations like,
“Do you think Jimmy Carter was our most underrated president?” as some random
seller eats chicken wings and halfheartedly listens in while hoping you’ll buy
something.

**RB:** In case you’re wondering, yes, I did buy the Rosalynn-Jimmy “I Helped
Put The Carters In The White House” pin (top left) and, yes, I do plan on
PhotoShopping Beyoncé and Jay Z over their faces.

## ![14700316319_7a539a3f97_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14700316319_7a539a3f97_o.jpg)

**BS:** This doesn’t seem like a form letter at all.

**RB:** There’s nothing better than ending a letter with “Good luck to you
and.” Nothing.

## ![14674934819_fa29531fb6_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674934819_fa29531fb6_o.jpg)

**BS:** If you already splurged on an authentic Charles Manson letter that
didn’t have a swastika and a “Deutsches Reich” label, seeing this had to hurt.

**RB:** I’ve got to say, not psyched at the sheer amount of Nazi memorabilia
in this convention. I respect one-upmanship in pretty much every arena, except
Nazi memorabilia.

## ![14674838830_0b4e81b471_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674838830_0b4e81b471_o.jpg)

**BS:** It’s hard to find Lenny Bias’s autograph (as you can tell by the
price). It’s also hard to recover from running back-to-back photos of Manson’s
Nazi letter and Lenny Bias. I’m depressed. What’s next? Cheer me up, next
photo!

## ![14858523471_091305f265_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14858523471_091305f265_o.jpg)

**RB:** Did you get this for the studio? I hope you did — it would look really
nice. Such history, all in one photo. What a moment.

**BS:** You’re half-right — I almost bought it for the studio, but I was going
to superimpose the photo of [A-Rod kissing
himself](http://cdn04.cdn.justjared.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/03/arod-
kisses/a-rod-kisses-himself-details-magazine-04.jpg) in the middle. I still
might.

## ![14674917429_17923a65e3_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674917429_17923a65e3_o.jpg)

**BS:** Conversation topics included “Macho Man played baseball???????,” “Holy
shit, he looked like a crazy cross between Henry Winkler and Vin Diesel!,” and
“They’re asking $600 for this???????”

**RB:** I want to publicly apologize to David “The Masked Man” Shoemaker for
not purchasing this and bringing it back to New York City. I have failed you,
friend.

## MOST DEPRESSING ITEMS

## ![14674915860_1f1b02a5bc_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674915860_1f1b02a5bc_o.jpg)

**BS:** You knew they sold game-worn memorabilia, but you never knew they sold
assassination-worn memorabilia, right? Instead of the JFK photo they picked,
why not show the Zapruder still of JFK’s head exploding from the bullet? Why
hold back? Why not make this the most tasteless convention item ever? Oh wait,
it already is? Then why not make it the most tasteless convention item
forever? I think they could have gone further. I need to take a shower.

**RB:** I hope no one ever buys this. With that said, I’m sure someone
purchased this 10 minutes after we walked away. Because everything’s terrible.

## ![14859096254_d283725b9c_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14859096254_d283725b9c_o.jpg)

**BS:** Backstreet Boys and New Kids on the Block. Tough sports year for
Boston.

**RB:** VICTORY. ALL IS RIGHT IN THE WORLD.

## ![14893157735_a05827acbd_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14893157735_a05827acbd_o.jpg)

**BS:** I thought about buying this, then immediately setting it on fire in
front of the person selling it. You’re gonna sell THAT photo of the Legend????
He’s the Basketball Jesus!

**RB:** Just for clarification, is it “Fat Bird” and “Magic” or “Fat (Bird and
Magic)” as in Magic is fat, alongside Fat Bird? Either way, I love this photo.

## ![14892834702_c47445c477_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14892834702_c47445c477_o.jpg)

**BS:** The JoePa market is a little slow right now.

**RB:** There’s no price, so I’m assuming these were just giveaways.

## ![14674921768_131e7b5493_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14674921768_131e7b5493_o.jpg)

**BS:** We picked this up because the non-Hank headlines were “SUICIDE: A
GROWING MENACE TO BLACK WOMEN” and “ADOPTION TRENDS: THE FIGHT FOR BLACK
BABIES,” thinking this had a chance to be the most depressing magazine ever
published …

## ![14881419673_d9dde3cff9_o](https://grantland.com/wp-
content/uploads/2014/08/14881419673_d9dde3cff9_o.jpg)

**BS:** But then we looked at the back and saw a guy smoking a cigarette while
eating an Italian ice. Don’t get down, black people of 1974! We have tons of
cigarettes and Italian ice!

**RB:** I think it’s more, “Hey, it’s 1974 and America still sucks for us. So
go have a cigarette. And eat some lime Italian ice. And then go play
basketball. You’ve earned it.”[ ](https://grantland.com/)
