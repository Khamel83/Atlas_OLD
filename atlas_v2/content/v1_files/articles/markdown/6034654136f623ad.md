The images can be sorted by century, color, genre, or library collection. And
to inspire further exploration and reuse, the library has also created a
number of digital games and tools, such as a "[mansion
builder](http://web.archive.org/web/20250722093335/http://publicdomain.nypl.org/mansion-
maniac/)" game, where users control a little blue Pac-man-like figure through
the floor plans of grand turn-of-the-century New York apartments.
