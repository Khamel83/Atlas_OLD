It’s nearing the end of summer, which both means that my personal schedule is becoming cramped, while tech news slows to a crawl; I’m going to take a personal day today, and also plan to take one next week, before ramping back up to a normal four days-a-week schedule.

There is, though, other content in the Stratechery Plus bundle, covering two recent stories about Facebook:
