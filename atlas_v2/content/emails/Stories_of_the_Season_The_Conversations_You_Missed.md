Earlier this month, in front of a packed
audience of awards season voters at The Brownstone in Los Angeles, four of Puck’s A-list journalists—Matthew Belloni, Lauren Sherman, Peter Hamby, and Baratunde Thurston—took the stage to conduct a series of intimate conversations with a slate of the season’s most distinguished filmmakers, costume designers, and actors. 

The premise of Puck’s inaugural Stories of the Season event was to elevate the
often dry and predictable awards season conversation series, and whether it was Peter’s riveting conversation with actors Jeremy Strong, Zoe Saldaña, and Peter Sarsgaard, or Matt Belloni’s keynote conversations with up-and-coming star Mikey Madison and Dune director Denis Villeneuve, the evening more than delivered on that promise. If you weren’t able to attend, you can
watch video replays of the panel conversations by clicking on the links below. The evening was presented in partnership with Polestar, and supported by Mayer Brown, Wondery, and HBO and Max.
