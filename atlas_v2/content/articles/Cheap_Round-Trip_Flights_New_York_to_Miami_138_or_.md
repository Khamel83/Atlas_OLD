### What If You’re Not in New York?

You can get to New York cheaply by using points ([like British Airways
Avios](http://web.archive.org/web/20150316175211/http://millionmilesecrets.com/2012/04/08/british-
airways-miles/)) to get there. British Airways Avios are [great for short-haul
flights](http://web.archive.org/web/20150316175211/http://millionmilesecrets.com/2015/02/03/book-
british-airways-award-flights-like-a-pro-part-11-the-best-domestic-awards-
using-british-airways-avios-points/) because it’s only 4,500 points 1-way for
flights less than 649 miles, and 7,500 points for flights up to 1,149 miles.

Or if you have the [Southwest Companion
Pass](http://web.archive.org/web/20150316175211/http://millionmilesecrets.com/2014/02/20/a-step-
by-step-guide-to-the-southwest-companion-pass/), you could fly to New York
very cheaply. That’s because with the Companion Pass, your companion flies
with you for almost free on both paid and award tickets.

Or you could try to find a cheap paid ticket from your home airport to New
York. But do the math to see if it’s worth it.
