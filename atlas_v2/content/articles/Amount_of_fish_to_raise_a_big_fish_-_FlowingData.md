# Amount of fish to raise a big fish

May 8, 2019

Raising living things requires resources. In the case of fish, it requires
more fish so that another can grow larger. [Artists Chow and Lin calculated
how
much](http://web.archive.org/web/20250616061548/https://www.chowandlin.com/fish).
The surrounding small fish are required to grow the three yellow carp in the
middle. [via
[kottke](http://web.archive.org/web/20250616061548/https://kottke.org/19/05/the-
ecological-footprint-of-fish)]
