There have been a lot of trailers for the upcoming *Batman: Arkham Knight*, detailing everything from [the weaponized new Batmobile](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207174612/http://www.theverge.com/2014/6/3/5775714/the-batmobile-is-a-missile-launching-arsenal-in-batman-arkham-knight) to some of the [new playable characters](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207174612/http://www.theverge.com/2015/4/27/8502735/batman-arkham-knight-dual-play-robin-nightwing-catwoman). But the latest gives perhaps the clearest look at the game yet: it's seven minutes of pure gameplay, letting you watch as Batman infiltrates a Scarecrow safe house and beats up a whole lot of thugs along the way. It doesn't look radically different from past *Arkham* games, but you do get some cool new gadgets to play with. *Arkham Knight* is launching on June 23rd for PS4, Xbox One, and PC — for more on the game, check out [*Polygon*'s new hands-on preview](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207174612/http://www.polygon.com/2015/5/28/8672915/batman-arkham-knight-buffet-of-gaming).
