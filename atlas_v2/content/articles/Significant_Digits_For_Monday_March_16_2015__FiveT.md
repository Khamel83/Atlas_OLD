## 10,000 years

How long a volcano needs to chill out before being described as “dormant.” The
distinction between an “active,” “dormant” and “extinct” volcano is actually
somewhat nuanced. Extinct volcanos are cut off from a magma supply, active
volcanos are, well, active, and dormant volcanoes haven’t erupted for at least
10,000 years (but with scientists believing it will erupt again). [[Mental
Floss](http://mentalfloss.com/article/62164/how-can-we-tell-when-volcano-
dormant-or-extinct)]

## $500,000 per episode

When Netflix scored the digital distribution rights for “Friends,” they paid
more than $500,000 per episode. Now that “Seinfeld” is on the block, the 180
episodes are reportedly going to catch an even higher price. While Netflix has
tapped out of the negotiations, Amazon, Yahoo and Hulu are reportedly still
in. [

[Wall Street Journal](http://blogs.wsj.com/cmo/2015/03/13/seinfeld-nears-
streaming-video-deal-yada-yada/)

]

## 117 million .coms

The “.com” moniker turned 30 on Sunday. March 15, 1985 was the day
manufacturer Symbolics decided to claim “Symbolics.com” as their web address,
the first of now 117 million dot-com websites.
[[VentureBeat](http://venturebeat.com/2015/03/14/30-years-of-com-domains/)]
