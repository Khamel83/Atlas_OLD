Grill marks. [Grill
marks](/web/20240302230820/https://food52.com/blog/10352-how-to-achieve-

fire, sometimes that steak just won't get those crosshatches of charred glory.
Or maybe you can't get the charcoal to light in the first place.

We've all been there—[some of
us](/web/20240302230820/https://food52.com/blog/13012-too-many-cooks-grilling-
successes-and-failures) more often than others. But there's just something
about grilling and summer that makes us want to be better grillers. They're a
dynamic duo, which is why, no matter your grilling woes, we're here with our
best grilling tips our community has written about over the years.
