The Barkley Treatise, Box Office Massacres, The Sephora Wars

Welcome back to The Daily Courant, your afternoon download of Puck’s best new reporting.

First up today, Dylan Byers unearths the latest drama enveloping the NBA rights auction: David Zaslav ’s $2.6 billion question, Inside the NBA ’s existential fears, the Charles Barkley of it all, and more.

Plus, below the fold: William D. Cohan takes a microscope to Sam Bankman-Fried ’s beef with Sullivan & Cromwell. Rachel Strugatz reveals the latest obsessions consuming beauty industry insiders. Tina Nguyen presents her wide-ranging conversation with conservative lobbyist Ralph Reed . On The Town , Matt Belloni and Lucas Shaw lament the disastrous Memorial Day box office weekend. And on The Powers That Be , Dylan and John Ourand dig into the nitty-gritty of the NBA broadcast rights debacle.
