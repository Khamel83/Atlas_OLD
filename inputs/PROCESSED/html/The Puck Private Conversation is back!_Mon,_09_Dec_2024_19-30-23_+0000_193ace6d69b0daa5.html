Dear reader,

Earlier this year, we launched the first quarterly installment of The Puck Private Conversation, powered by Orchestra, the strategy and communications mega-agency, as yet another way to deliver on one of the founding principles at Puck: to reveal what executives, decision-makers, and our elite readership really think about the high-level questions and problems affecting the industries we cover so closely.

Today, I’m thrilled to announce the second installment of this groundbreaking series. This time, we’re focusing on how executives and industry insiders are navigating the post-election economic landscape: the potential impact of tariffs, trade wars, deregulation, changing tax regimes, and a hyper-charged political climate. We’re also diving more deeply into how our audience of media consumers and producers, alike, are engaging with an entertainment industry and information space that is more fractured than ever.

But remember, this is meant to be fun . The anonymous survey only takes a few minutes to complete, and just like the first time, your responses will be synthesized by my partner Baratunde Thurston , who will sift through the data and surface the most relevant and surprising insights. This data provides the sort of landscape analysis that other companies outsource to consulting firms. Here at Puck, it’s just another benefit of membership. Thanks for your time, and I hope you’re as excited as I am to dig into the resultss.
