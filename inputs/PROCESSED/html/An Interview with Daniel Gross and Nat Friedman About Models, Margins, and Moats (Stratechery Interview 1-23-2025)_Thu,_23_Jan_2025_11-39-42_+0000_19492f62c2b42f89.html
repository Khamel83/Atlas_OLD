Good morning,

I am happy to welcome Daniel Gross and Nat Friedman back for a Stratechery Interview, the eighth in our ongoing series (we previously talked in October 2022 , December 2022 , March 2023 , August 2023 , December 2023 , February 2024 , and June 2024 ).

This series is somewhat unique for Stratechery in that my interview subjects are generally not investors; this series, though, started in October 2022, when Friedman and Gross were launching a grant program, precisely because we shared a sense that there wasn't nearly enough activity or discussion around AI; one month later ChatGPT came out and the world has changed dramatically, including for Friedman and Gross, who are two of the leading investors in the space. Gross is also now the CEO of Safe Superintelligence Inc., which we do not discuss in this interview. To that end, I think it has been very valuable — and popular — to continue this series, but do keep in mind that Friedman and Gross may be invested in some of the companies we discuss.

We also, as is our wont, get into some more philosophical questions, including the implications of believing in AGI, the importance of personality, and the battle for the future of language and culture inherent in these models.
