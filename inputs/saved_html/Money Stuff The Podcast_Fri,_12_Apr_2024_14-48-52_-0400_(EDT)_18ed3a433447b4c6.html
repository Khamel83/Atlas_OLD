Programming note: As I mentioned yesterday, we’re trying a new Money Stuff podcast, in which my friend Katie Greifeld and I discuss the week in the newsletter and financial news. If you like the newsletter but want more of it, in podcast form, check out the podcast.

Our first episode is out now. This week, we discuss the Destiny Tech100 fund, the Mango Markets exploit and the Bureau of Labor Statistics inflation super users. Also there’s a reader creative-writing submission? It was fun. You can listen to the episode here .

We’d love your feedback. You can email us at moneypod@bloomberg.net .

You can get Money Stuff: The Podcast at Apple, Spotify or wherever you get your podcasts. New episodes will come out on Fridays.

Also, because people have asked: The podcast is just a fun extra thing we are doing. If you prefer your Money Stuff in traditional newsletter form, nothing will change for you. Except, I guess, that I am currently sending you this email.
