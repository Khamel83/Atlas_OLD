Dear Reader,

We wanted to make sure you had a chance to listen to our podcasts, which connect you even more intimately to our incredible team of journalists and the inside conversations that they are having.

The Powers That Be

PETER HAMBY

Emmy Award-winning journalist Peter Hamby, along with our team of expert writers, bring you smart, elite conversations about the inside story at the intersection of Wall Street, Washington, Silicon Valley and Hollywood.

The Town

MATTHEW BELLONI

Puck founding partner Matthew Belloni takes you inside Hollywood, using exclusive reporting and insight to explain the backstories on everything from Marvel movies to ‘The Bachelor.’ Three times per week, Matt will touch on what is getting made and why, who is winning and losing, and what people in show business are actually talking about.

Somebody’s Gotta Win

TARA PALMERI

Puck senior political correspondent Tara Palmeri is here to help you get through what may be the most chaotic and consequential presidential election cycle of our lifetime. With 15 years of experience covering politics, Tara speaks with the smartest political brains to discuss what is happening behind the scenes in Washington D.C., and on the campaign trail.

About a Boy: The Story of Vladimir Putin

JULIA IOFFE

Puck founding partner and Washington correspondent Julia Ioffe, “About a Boy” is a five-part documentary podcast series exploring the forces that shaped Putin’s childhood and how they inform his presidency and strategy in Ukraine.
