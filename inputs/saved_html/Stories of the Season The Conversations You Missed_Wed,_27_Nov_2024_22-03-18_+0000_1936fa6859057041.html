The premise of Puck’s inaugural Stories of the Season event was to elevate the often dry and predictable awards season conversation series, and whether it was Peter’s riveting conversation with actors Jeremy Strong, Zoe Saldaña, and Peter Sarsgaard, or Matt Belloni’s keynote conversations with up-and-coming star Mikey Madison and Dune director Denis Villeneuve, the evening more than delivered on that promise.

Earlier this month, in front of a packed audience of awards season voters at The Brownstone in Los Angeles, four of Puck’s A-list journalists— Matthew Belloni , Lauren Sherman , Peter Hamby , and Baratunde Thurston —took the stage to conduct a series of intimate conversations with a slate of the season’s most distinguished filmmakers, costume designers, and actors.

The premise of Puck’s inaugural Stories of the Season event was to elevate the often dry and predictable awards season conversation series, and whether it was Peter’s riveting conversation with actors Jeremy Strong , Zoe Saldaña , and Peter Sarsgaard , or Matt Belloni ’s keynote conversations with up-and-coming star Mikey Madison and Dune director Denis Villeneuve , the evening more than delivered on that promise. If you weren’t able to attend, you can watch video replays of the panel conversations by clicking on the links below. The evening was presented in partnership with Polestar, and supported by Mayer Brown, Wondery, and HBO and Max.

Event Lineup

Keynote Q&A: A Live Recording of ‘The Town’

HOSTED BY

MATTHEW BELLONI

An exclusive and intimate conversation between Matt Belloni and Dune filmmaker Denis Villeneuve about his unique process and how he views the evolution of the entertainment business. The Keynote Q&A was followed by the Breakthrough Performer Q&A with actress Mikey Madison of Anora . Both conversations were recorded for an episode of The Town .

SPEAKERS

Denis Villeneuve , Mikey Madison

Going There: The Fearless Performances

HOSTED BY

PETER HAMBY

Peter Hamby brought together three standout actors— Peter Sarsgaard , Jeremy Strong , and Zoe Saldaña —whose performances made particularly bold statements about the world. They discussed their creative process, how they choose their roles, and how their movies engage with real-life narratives and the broader culture.

Slaying the Screen: The Fashion and Costumes of the Season

HOSTED BY

LAUREN SHERMAN

Lauren Sherman gathered four of the top costume designers in awards contention for a lively chat about the fashion behind some of the season’s most talked-about films.
