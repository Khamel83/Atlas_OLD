THE INBOX IS THE NEW HOME SCREEN

The inbox is where itГўВҖВҷs really at. I am, of course, heavily biased, but I feel WeChatГўВҖВҷs is the best in class. RISE OF THE TORTILLA CHIP APP

This term ГўВҖВ“ ГўВҖВңappГўВҖВқ ГўВҖВ“ is rather old, yet only entered common parlance with the proliferation of smartphones. This is no coincidence. Not only this, but adding people could be far better. Connectivity ГўВҖВ” I wrote before about how apps here get around peopleГўВҖВҷs reluctance to use their data plans. So the meta-platforms ГўВҖВ” WeChat, Facebook, LINE, and the like ГўВҖВ” have come and addressed many of the pain points above. TheyГўВҖВҷve delivered solutions neither the open web nor those behind the closed app store model were coordinated enough, thoughtful enough, or perhaps incentivized enough to produce.

And though theyГўВҖВҷre still quite far from wholly replacing the high-level features OS offer to users and app developers, we can clearly see the beginning of this encroachment.

So here we are. What do we do?

I donГўВҖВҷt know about you, but hereГўВҖВҷs what I want to see happen.
