Good morning,

Today is Chinese New Year — 新年快樂！— and while this was originally scheduled to be a day off, there are a host of Big Tech earnings coming out today and tomorrow. I'm particularly interested in management commentary given the news of the last week, so I'm making a (very rare!) scheduling adjustment: tomorrow I will publish an Update likely covering Meta and/or Microsoft, and then publish the weekly Stratechery Interview on Friday.

Second, I wanted to apologize again for anyone that had issues last week with the updates to Stratechery. This was, as you might have surmised, a pretty massive update that extended far beyond the website redesign, including significant database changes to Passport. That part actually went fine — the latest version has been running on SemiAnalysis for a couple of months — but we did run into several interlocking issues that were related to previous migrations. There were also a few bugs that only manifested at Stratechery scale, including some CDN issues, some issues related to how the Stratechery bundle works, and maddeningly, a third party library update to our UUID encoding library that dropped characters for IDs that started 00. It was very much a "when it rains it pours" situation.

Regardless, I did not deliver the user experience that I want to, and I'm sorry.

What is particularly disappointing is that these bugs may have overshadowed the biggest news surrounding the upgrade, which was the addition of the Asianometry podcast and newsletter to the Stratechery Plus bundle. Please do check out my introductory post , if you missed it, as well as the Interview I did with Jon Yu . I'm thrilled to have Jon on board, and wish him and you a happy year of the snake! See you tomorrow.
