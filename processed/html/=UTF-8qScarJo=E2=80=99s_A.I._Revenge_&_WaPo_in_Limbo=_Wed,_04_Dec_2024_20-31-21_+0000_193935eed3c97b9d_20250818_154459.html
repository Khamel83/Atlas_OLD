Secret Gartenfeld, Ourand vs. Marchand, Hegseth Blowback

Welcome back to The Daily Courant, your afternoon digest featuring Puck’s best new reporting.

First up today, Eriq Gardner spotlights a first-of-its-kind class-action lawsuit, fronted by actor Paul Lehrman , alleging that A.I. voice generator Lovo illegally cloned his voice—along with Barack Obama ’s, Conan O’Brien ’s, Elton John ’s, and other bold-faced names. An imminent legal decision could upend an entire industry.

Plus, below the fold: Marion Maneker chats with ICA Miami’s wunderkind director, Alex Gartenfeld , about transforming Miami into a contemporary art mecca. Abby Livingston reveals how the various congressional cafeteria tables are assessing Trump ’s cabinet noms. On The Varsity , John Ourand reunites with The Athletic’s Andrew Marchand to celebrate his starring role in Puck’s cinematic universe, ESPN’s Pat McAfee strategy, and the escalating sports streaming wars. On Somebody’s Gotta Win , Tara Palmeri rings up lobbyist Joe Hack to download all the Beltway chatter surrounding Trump’s nominees. And on The Powers That Be , Dylan Byers joins Peter Hamby to diagnose the latest migraines at The Washington Post as Will Lewis attempts to reconfigure the newsroom.

Marchand!
