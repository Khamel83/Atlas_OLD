Hakeem on Trump, WaPo Nail-Biting, Belichick’s Second Act

Welcome back to The Daily Courant, your afternoon dose of Puck’s best new reporting.

First up today, Dylan Byers explains what Politico poaching NBC’s Dasha Burns says about the dismal state of television news, the uncertainty inside 30 Rock, and the future of Politico, itself. Then Dylan provides the latest readout from across the Potomac, where Washington Post staffers are still waiting for Will Lewis to announce a “change agent” editor to lead the newsroom.

Plus, below the fold: Rachel Strugatz considers whether Rhode, Hailey Bieber ’s nine-figure beauty business, can avoid Goop’s “key man risk.” John Heilemann sits down with House Democratic leader Hakeem Jeffries to discuss the future of bipartisanship under Trump . Baratunde Thurston connects with Eriq Gardner , in a special conversation presented by Meta, to reveal how Hollywood is countenancing the rise of A.I. On Somebody’s Gotta Win , Tara Palmeri rings up veterans advocate Paul Rieckhoff to unpack Pete Hegseth ’s charm campaign. And on The Powers That Be , John Ourand joins Peter Hamby for a candid chat about the booming college football business and Bill Belichick ’s path to Chapel Hill.
