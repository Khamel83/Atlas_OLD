### Chase Sapphire Reserve 100,000 Point Bonus Ending

There’s Just Over a Week Left, Then the Chase Sapphire Reserve Bonus Will Be Cut in Half!

Currently, when you open the **[Chase Sapphire Reserve](https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/ChaseSapphireReserve100K201611/?amp=1)** card, you’ll earn **100,000 Chase Ultimate Rewards points** after you spend $4,000 on purchases in the first 3 months from account opening.  

You’ll also get:

* **3X Chase Ultimate Rewards** points on travel & dining
* **1X Chase Ultimate Rewards** points on all other purchases
* **$300 annual credit**for travel purchases such as airfare and hotels
* **$100 statement credit**for [Global Entry](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/get-a-same-day-walk-in-appointment-at-these-global-entry-enrollment-centers/?amp=1)
* [Priority Pass Select](https://www.prioritypass.com/en/Select) for access to [airport lounges](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/are-airport-lounges-worth-it/?amp=1)
* No foreign transaction fees
* [Visa Infinite](https://www.visa.com/infinite/) benefits like $25 food and beverage credit at the [Luxury Hotel Collection](https://www.visaluxuryhotelcollection.com/) and complimentary car rental elite status with Silvercar

Starting **January 12, 2017**, the sign-up bonus will be reduced to **50,000 Chase Ultimate Rewards points** after meeting the same minimum spending requirement.

However, according to [The Points Guy](https://thepointsguy.com/2017/01/chase-sapphire-reserve-100k-online-bonus-ending/), you’ll still be able to earn the higher bonus if you **apply in-branch before March 12, 2017.**

### What If You’re Not Eligible?

**Link:   [What to Do If You’re Not Eligible for the Chase Sapphire Reserve Bonus](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/cant-get-chase-sapphire-reserve/?amp=1)**

Because the [Chase Sapphire Reserve](https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/ChaseSapphireReserve100K201611/?amp=1) falls under the [Chase “5/24” rule](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/chase-5-24-rule/?amp=1), [many folks who’ve been in the miles and points hobby for a while aren’t eligible for the card](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/cant-get-chase-sapphire-reserve/?amp=1).

That means if you’ve opened ~5+ cards from **any** bank ([except certain business cards](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/best-amex-business-cards/?amp=1)) in the past 24 months, it’s unlikely you’ll be [approved for most Chase cards](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/chase-5-24-rule/?amp=1).

That said, [some folks have had luck being pre-approved for the card in-branch](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/chase-sapphire-reserve-approval-tips/?amp=1), even if they’re above 5/24.

### Bottom Line

The [100,000 point sign-up bonus](https://millionmilesecrets.com/reviews/chase-sapphire-reserve/?amp=1) on the [Chase Sapphire Reserve](https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/ChaseSapphireReserve100K201611/?amp=1) will end **January 11, 2017,** for **online applications**.

It will be cut in half, to **50,000 Chase Ultimate Rewards points**, after [meeting the same minimum spending requirement](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/how-to-meet-credit-card-minimum-spend/?amp=1).

If you’ve had your eye on the card, you’ve got just over a week to apply online and earn the higher bonus.  However, [The Points Guy](https://thepointsguy.com/2017/01/chase-sapphire-reserve-100k-online-bonus-ending/) reports the better bonus will **still** be available in-branch until **March 12, 2017**.
