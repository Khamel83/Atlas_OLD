I think that’s why the market was so strong last week. But let’s step back and take a deep breath. In terms of how much this will impact the media space, that’s where it gets more difficult. A lot of these companies have assets that the Trump administration doesn’t love—everything from Google and **Mark Zuckerberg** to CBS and CNN. So, what will that mean in terms of consolidation? I don’t know. There is a lot of change afoot, and it’s all because the traditional media businesses—meaning TV—are in secular decline, so everybody wants to merge.
