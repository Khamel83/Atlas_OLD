### What’s Hyatt Diamond Status?

**Link:   [Hyatt Diamond Elite Status](https://www.hyatt.com/gp/en/benefits/membership_diamond.jsp)**

With [Hyatt Diamond status](https://www.hyatt.com/gp/en/benefits/membership_diamond.jsp) you get:

* 30% bonus on points earned for stays
* Best room available on arrival (excluding suites)
* Free premium internet
* Lounge access with free breakfast and evening appetizers (free full breakfast at Hyatt hotels without lounges)
* 4 suite upgrades (up to 7 consecutive nights each) per year
* Late check-out at 4:00 pm upon request
* 2 one-time use United Airlines lounge club passes each year

### How Do You Complete the Hyatt Diamond Challenge?

To enroll in the Hyatt Diamond Challenge, call Hyatt at **800-228-3360**.

You’ll then have **60 days to stay 12 nights at Hyatt hotels** to earn [Hyatt Diamond status](https://www.hyatt.com/gp/en/benefits/membership_diamond.jsp) through **February 2017**.  That’s much better than the 25 stays or 50 nights you’d need if you didn’t take the Challenge.

[Points + Cash stays](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/hyatt-points-cash-is-a-triple-win/?amp=1) count towards Diamond status and [it’s easy to earn lots of Hyatt points](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/how-to-earn-hyatt-points/%20?amp=1).  So you can save money on your 12 nights.

With the old Hyatt Diamond Challenge you received Diamond status while you were working towards your 12 nights.  But now you only get [Hyatt Platinum status](https://www.hyatt.com/gp/en/benefits/membership_platinum.jsp):

* 15% bonus points when you stay at Hyatt
* Preferred room (higher floor or larger room)
* Free premium internet
* Late check-out at 2:00 pm upon request

And if you only complete 6 nights of the challenge you get to keep the Platinum status.  You’ll also earn **1,000 Hyatt bonus points for your 1st 6 nights**.

**Note:** You get Platinum status for free if you have the [Chase Hyatt](https://millionmilesecrets.com/go/Hyatt2FreeNights/?amp=1) card.  So cardholders should only do the challenge if they know they’ll stay 12 Hyatt nights in 60 days to earn Diamond status.

### Bottom Line

The new Hyatt Diamond Status Challenge is now open to everyone.  To sign-up call Hyatt at **800-228-3360.**

Once you complete 12 nights at Hyatt hotels (paid or [Points + Cash](https://millionmilesecrets.com/guides/hyatt-points-cash-is-a-triple-win/?amp=1)), you’ll earn [Diamond Status](https://www.hyatt.com/gp/en/benefits/membership_diamond.jsp) through **February 2017!**

Hyatt Diamond status is 1 of the best hotel perks available!  I love having **free full breakfasts** and **4 suite upgrades each year**.  So if you stay at Hyatt hotels frequently, this is a terrific deal!
