## re/spin

[The re/spin service](http://resp.in/) helps you import any Spotify or Last.fm playlist into Rdio. Unfortunately, I have a feeling that before too long, we’ll need a service to convert Rdio collections and playlists to Spotify. (via [@capndesign](https://twitter.com/capndesign))
