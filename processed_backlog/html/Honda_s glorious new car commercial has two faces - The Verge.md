## Symmetrical videos show Honda's dark and light side

By  [Dante D'Orazio](/web/20241207091625/https://www.theverge.com/authors/dante-dorazio)

Via [Jalopnik](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207091625/http://jalopnik.com/this-brilliant-honda-civic-type-r-ad-is-secretly-two-fi-1652973369) | Source [Wieden + Kennedy London Blog](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207091625/http://wklondon.typepad.com/welcome_to_optimism/2014/10/new-civic-type-r-film-brings-to-life-hondas-other-side.html)

Honda's latest ad goes through brilliant — and beautiful — lengths to prove that the Japanese car company doesn't just make boring cars that can take a beating. The short film, called "The Other Side," shows the dichotomy between Honda's dependable cars and its racing pedigree.

One half of the tale follows a seemingly normal father picking up his daughters from school. But the other half reveals a bit more: that friendly dad is actually a getaway driver by night. Both 3-minute videos are wonderfully shot, and you can seamlessly switch between them during playback by hitting the "R" key. That's "R" as in the [Honda Civic Type R](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207091625/http://jalopnik.com/the-276-hp-honda-civic-type-r-will-be-the-most-extreme-1640256870), the dashing red hot hatch that serves as the getaway car in the clip. Be sure to watch to the end: not everything is as it seems.

To watch the full ad, you will have [to visit Honda's YouTube page](https://web.archive.org/web/20241207091625/https://www.hondatheotherside.com/). The video above is just a trailer.
